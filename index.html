Quick Message from an Oxnard Native

My name is Richard Le and I grew up in Oxnard. In the past few years Oxnard has grown to be a great town. It’s safe, has diverse food options, and is rapidly growing. If you ever come here you have to check out Topper’s Pizza Place. It’s one of the best pizza places I’ve ever had. Nothing beats the quick lunch.
